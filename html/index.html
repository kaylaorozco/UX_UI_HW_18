My UX Skills

User Research

One of my main passions is researching. I love the depth that research can add to any project I am working on. Research can be effective in my work by allowing my to empathize with users and see things from their persepctive.

Wireframing

The core process of wireframing is a key section of design that I enjoy. Wireframing provides the "skeleton" of my projects that allows me to visualize, based off of my research, the end product of the mobile app or website.

Storytelling
